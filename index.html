My First Few Weeks of USERDES Class

Before I took this class, when I heard a term Design, I imagined visual or looks. But now,
this idea has changed. Design is not only about how it looks like. It also means how it works and how it feels like. If I think about it, it really makes sense since I considered about functionality when I use or buy any products. Moreover, functionality is more important than visuals personally. What I got impressed the most is that Design is solving problems. It did not make sense for me for seconds after hearing this. Solving problems leads to improve functionality. That also means making design better.

Making personas was also new idea to me. I knew a way to get feedback from users and
reflect the user’s experience, but I have never thought to create virtual users specifically. First, I thought it is better to target wide than targeting specific users. But creating something everyone's like makes products plain, rather than that it is better to target specific users’ needs. If you have personas, you can think from the user’s perspective and avoid wrong assumptions. You can also clarify users' needs. Even if you target specific persona, the products might meet many people’s needs as a result. I learned that meeting user’s needs is very important when designing products.

Introduction to Design Thinking Process

When I heard Design Thinking Process, I thought it is just about a creating a plan. But in
fact, the importance of design thinking process is to meet the needs of users. Design Thinking Process has five stages: Empathise, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. Empathising with the users instead of just thinking about them was a novel idea for me. It is interesting way to know the needs of the users. There are things that might be noticed by thinking the feelings of the users instead of thinking as a designer. After this process, the needs of the users will be defined. I understand that defining a specific user need is essential to think a solution. If the user need is not set, the purpose will be blurred.

I was assuming it is important to have a unique and nice solution, but it is also important
to come up with as many ideas as possible for the user needs. Even though an idea is unique, it is not a good idea if it does not satisfy the user’s need. That is why I it would be better to come up with a solution by focusing on the good points or strong points of many ideas. I believe design thinking process is to solve a problem and achieve a goal based on a user needs and feedback from the beginning to the end. I think doing the process by focusing on the user leads to a good result for the user.

Need Finding (Design Problem)

First, our group tried to design a problem from our experience and situation nowadays.
After that, we combined the ideas and created a problem statement. Later, I realized how
important to state a clear problem statement. Our first statement had multiple problems to solve because we combined the problems. It was not focusing on the specific problem. Therefore, it was hard to come up with solutions. After we stated the revised problem statement that focuses on one problem, we were also able to focus on a problem properly in order to come up with solutions.

I also learned that the problem statement would be better to have a keyword related to the
solution and not too vague. I thought the problem statement should be generalized but it was actually opposite. The problem statement should pinpoint the specific problem. Since the problem statement will be a basis through every process of the project, it should be cleared and defined the problem right. In our group case, we are going to add a method name of the solution to the problem statement.

Thinking how the app would be used by personas was interesting. Personas have a
common problem but different situations. Thinking the pain points of each personas helps to make the problem clearer. It was fun to think how the personas feel about the problem and why they want to solve it. Additionally, how the solution we came up with would help the personas.

Information Architecture

I have been visited tons of websites but I realized that I was led to the information
that I wanted by information architecture without noticing. I never cared about it but all
information in sites is organized in many ways and easy to search. I have visited to a site
that I had a hard time to reach to a page that I wanted. Now I learned that it was a matter
of information architecture. I learned that designing sites is not only about a visual but
also an information architecture.

One of the important factors of information architecture is a navigation. Now I
think about it, when I visit a website that I visit for the first time, I check if there is any
navigation to find a page that I want. Navigation is also important for visitors who get to
a page from a link or a search. Not everyone gets to a site from a home page. Therefore,
every page in a site should have name of the site and navigation for other pages.
I believe that if sites are related to e-commerce, information architecture is very
important for sales. How easy the users can get to a page depends on navigations in a site.

In my opinion, recommending related pages is one of good strategies of e-commerce
sites. When I read an online article, I check recommended articles at the end of the page
and read when I get curious. I do the same action at online shopping sites too. I believe
that good information architecture is always user-friendly.

Visual Information Design

First, I learned that visual is very important factors to deliver information or contents.
The impressions that visual gives to viewers changes depending on the design even though the contents do not change. I believe typography would be a big part of visual design since most websites are made up of letters. Images, videos, and pictures are also used but users eventually see the text to get information. Typography makes the text easier to read and understand by adjusting the font type, character size, line spacing and alignment etc. This means that typography involves all textual information. If the texts are difficult to read, it will be burden to users. In this kind of case, user might leave before they read the texts properly, Therefore, typography is important in order to deliver information to users.

What I learned new from the lecture is Grid Systems. I knew that layout is important to
design anything, but I did not know that there is a way to make alignments using grids. I believe it is logical and effective way to create a layout compared to a layout which is created by just a feeling. Even complex information can be easy to read by arranging and ordering with grid system.

Lastly, color is the most familiar design tool in my opinion. We always consider about
color when we draw something, when we wear cloths, and when we buy any products etc. This is because every color gives different impression even with the same design. Not only the impression of a single color, but also the combination of colors creates another impression. Therefore, I believe it is important to avoid using a color that is far from the image of contents. It is also essential to choose a color that is easy to read. I will be careful about what I learned from this topic from now on.
